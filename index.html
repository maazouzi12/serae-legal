A note on trust

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 6, 2026 · Last updated: June 6, 2026

SERAE is built with respect for your privacy. This policy explains, in plain language, what information the app uses and what happens to it.

What we collect

To give you an account and a practice shaped around you, SERAE collects a small amount of information:

  • Your email address, when you create an account.
  • Your mood check-ins, to shape your sessions and your daily letter.
  • Your journal entries, which are private to you.
  • Your session history and streak, to track your practice.

We use no advertising, and we do not use tracking tools that identify you for advertisers.

What stays on your device

Most of your practice lives on your device: your journal, your streak, your preferences, your chosen voice, and your progress. If you create an account, this information also syncs to a private record tied to your account, so it is there if you change phones. You can export your journal, or delete everything, at any time from Settings.

How your data is used

To deliver core features, SERAE relies on a few trusted service providers. Each receives only what it needs to do its job, and never your identity for advertising:

  • An authentication provider, to create and secure your account and sync your practice across devices.
  • A payment provider, together with Apple, to manage your membership.
  • An AI provider, which writes your personalized daily letter. To do this, a small amount of context (such as your mood, your streak, and a short excerpt of a recent reflection) is sent so the letter can be written. It is processed under that provider's privacy policy and is not used to train their models.
  • A secure server, which stores the limited data needed to run features such as the daily letter, and to receive feedback you choose to send.

Your meditations play from audio bundled in the app, so the sessions themselves are not generated from your personal data.

What we never do

We never sell your data. We never show you ads. We never share your mental-health data with advertisers. We never use your data to train AI models.

Subscriptions

Memberships are processed by Apple through StoreKit. SERAE never sees or stores your payment details. You can manage or cancel a subscription any time in your App Store account settings.

Your rights

You can export your journal entries whenever you wish. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Account → Delete my account. This removes the data associated with your account from our systems.

Children

SERAE is designed for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will appear at this URL with a new date at the top.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the developer at the email listed on the App Store listing.
