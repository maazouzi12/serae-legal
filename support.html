Support

Whatever you need, here's how to find it.

Contact us. For help with SERAE, contact the developer at the email listed on the App Store listing. We read every message, and we answer with care.

Managing your membership

You can manage or cancel your subscription any time from your iPhone: Settings → your name → Subscriptions → SERAE. Inside the app, you can also go to Settings → Account & Membership.

Restoring a purchase

New phone, or reinstalled the app? Open Settings → Account & Membership → Restore Purchases and your membership returns.

Your free trial and billing

New members begin with a 14-day free trial, then the plan you chose. Nothing is charged during the trial. You can cancel any time, at least 24 hours before the trial ends, and you will not be billed.

Deleting your account

You can delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Account → Delete my account. This is permanent.

A note on care

SERAE is for wellness and relaxation. It is not medical advice or treatment. If you are in distress, please reach a trained person who can help: in the US, call or text 988; in the UK, call the Samaritans at 116 123; or find a line near you at findahelpline.com.
